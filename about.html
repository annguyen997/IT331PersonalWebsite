My brief thoughts on certain topics.
Likes Dislikes Questions Conclusion
Education An opportunity to learn new skills that can benefit personally and professionally. Learning, especially in a formal setting like school, can be stressful, and sometimes unforgiving. Is academics embracing future technologies efficiently enough? Education is one of the most important elements of the human condition. Every person has the power to make academics great.
Travel The opportunity to explore new possibilities and perspectives, which include new cultures, languages, and customs. There is always an possibility for risk in new places, and traveling takes planning and funding. How can we convey our traveling experiences to others in a way that is compelling? Planned and done properly, traveling the world can be a wonderful experience to grow as an individual.
Health As health is a very important priority for me, there are many opportunities to maintain and improve one's physical health and overall well-being, which includes group exericse sessions, cooking, meditation, and more. There are many aspects regarding well-being, which means it takes work to find a balance that works for me. Are there any new frontiers in health that I should look for? Health is valuable, and it takes lots of commitment and responsibility.
Politics/Civil Participation In the United States at least, all individuals have the right to express their ideas on issues, and that could be beneficial for the future of democray and its values. There is always the potential for conflicting ideas, which can determential to progress. How do we approach issues civilly and with open minds (i.e. bipartisan) in today's environment? It is important to participate in our government and its operations. Laws and policies can have a significant impact on people's lives.
